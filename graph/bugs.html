Known Bugs and Problems with V2.2

  1. The ParseFunction class fails to pass the exponent of the power operator correctly. The solution is to place parenthesis () around the offending text to force precidence.
  2. The parse function class cannot parse intrinsic functions with more than two parameters.
  3. Special function class Statistic functions are incomplete.
  4. On the Mac. the XOR of 2 colors does not seem to work correctly. This means that the rubberband drag rectangle looks interesting to say the least.
